About Creatures of War

Creatures of War makes no judgments on the ethics of the exploitation of animals in warfare itself, this website is simply a narrow presentation of few cases of it happening. With that said, everybody can agree that the less harm that comes to animal is all the better. Please consider writing your elected officials regarding the loose protections granted to animals and their habitats outlined in the geneva convention and urge them to make it official national policy wherever you may reside.
