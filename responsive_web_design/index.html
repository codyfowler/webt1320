See this bear?

This is Wojteck (pronounced voy-tek). He, like some other animals, share a rare espirit de corps not experienced by most, in that they served as part of or along side a military force. Officially conscripted by the Polish Army, Wojteck traveled alongside the 22nd Artillery Company where he boosted morale and according to a report of a british soldier there was at least one case of him transporting munitions.

Wojtek is the prime exam of what I would like for you to briefly glance at on this site. Please paruse this limited site, with it's small showing of animal involved in war through happenstance (or otherwise).
